To get the datapack in your world:
1. choose the world in the singleplayer menu in-game and click on the "Edit" button.
2. choose "Open World Folder" to get to your world folder.
3. get into the "datapacks" folder and insert the zip file of the datapack into the folder.
4. get into the world and type in the chat "/reload" and press Enter to activate the datapack.

Enjoy!
